ABOUT US

Wearmouth Canada Inc. was founded in 1951 by Bill Wearmouth and has over 60 years of experience in the decommissioning, deconstruction, dismantling, demolition and recycling industries. We take pride in the development of safe, environmentally preferred solutions to meet your project or service needs.

With our experienced team of professionals we are continuously investigating and developing new ways to improve our services for our many clients throughout Western Canada.

SERVICES

Wearmouth Canada provides a wide array of services for our customers throughout the Alberta area.



DEMOLITION AND DISMANTLING SERVICES

Wearmouth Canada provides safe and efficient demolition and dismantling services for buildings and structures of all sizes.

  • Alberta Construction Safety Association Recognized Safety Program since 1993

  • Independently owned

  • Industrial 60, 100, and 120 cubic yard demolition trailers, end dumps, push outs and walking floors

  • Bridge span retrieval system

  • Unique dismantling / demolition safe work practices including post tensioned concrete

  • Designed and operated Western Canada's 1st Calgary Area Hazardous Waste Transfer Station

  • Initiated and sponsored concrete recycling studies with the University of Calgary

  • Long Standing Members of the National Association of Demolition Contractors
